About HandyMan Connect

A platform built to empower local skilled workers and simplify home services for everyone.

Our Mission

Connecting Skill With Need

HandyMan Connect was created with a simple goal: make it easy for homeowners to find trusted, skilled local workers — and make it easy for those workers to find consistent, well-paying work.

In UK, millions of skilled craftsmen — plumbers, electricians, carpenters, cleaners — struggle to find customers while homeowners face the challenge of locating trusted help. We bridge that gap with technology.

Our platform is transparent, fair, and community-driven. Workers keep full control of their pricing and schedule. Customers get verified, reviewed professionals at their doorstep.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Trust & Safety

Every worker is verified with ID proof and background checks. Customer safety is our top priority at all times.

Fairness

Workers set their own rates. No hidden platform fees. Transparent pricing so customers know what to expect.

Empowerment

We help local workers build their reputation, grow their client base, and earn a sustainable income with dignity.

Quality First

Every service interaction is rated. Low-rated workers are reviewed. We maintain a high standard for every job.

Community

We invest in local communities by giving workers the tools to succeed and homeowners peace of mind.

Accessibility

Simple, mobile-friendly design means anyone — worker or customer — can use our platform without barriers.
